About Integral Coach Factory

The Integral Coach Factory is one of the earliest production units of independent India. It was inaugurated by the first Prime Minister of India Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru on 2nd October, 1955. Later the Furnishing Division was inaugurated on 2nd October, 1962 and the production of fully furnished coaches steadily increased over the years. Spread over nearly 473 acres, it has about 11,095 workers toiling in harsh conditions to make more than 2,000 coaches every year which includes conventional and self propelled coaches.

Our Vision

To be the leader in rail coach manufacturing and to continually improve our products to meet global standards.

Production

ICF is capable of manufacturing many types / variants simultaneously. Every year new variants are added to the product mix. ICF invented and experimented with new type coaches whether conventional, self propelled or special type coaches. ICF has produced more than 54131 coaches since its inception in approximately 500 different designs.

Recent Achievements

ICF has turned out a record outturn of 2277 coaches during the year 2016-17 consisting of more than 50 variants involving high technological inputs, meticulous planning and execution. The coaches includes 1,162 stainless steel coaches. ICF will completely switch over to manufacture of latest design, safer and faster stainless steel coaches from the next production year.

ICF had bagged the Railway Minister's Shield for Environment for 2015-16. ICF secured Second prize in National Energy Conservation Award, 2016 under Hospital Sector. Chennai Rail Museum has seen extensive improvements and two more galleries added to showcase rich cultural heritage of Railways. Special emphasis has been given for Go Green movement and cleanliness, both inside factory premises and outside, and ICF has been rated as "GreenCo Silver" under GreenCo Rating System.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective rail coaches while ensuring customer satisfaction and safety.

Export

ICF has bagged an export order from Srilanka for supply of 78 DEMU coaches. ICF will also be manufacturing prestigious state of the art 160 kmph speed train set, called "Train-18".

Go Green

ICF is self sufficient in its energy requirements and is the only unit of Indian Railways meeting all energy requirements through the renewable source of energy, the wind energy. ICF is a Zero discharge factory and also a 'Green Workshop'. ICF has adopted an "Environment Policy' to operate the factory in an environmental friendly manner for a safe, harmonious and ecologically balanced environment around it. ICF has introduced the Bio-discharge toilets in all the coaches turned out from ICF from April 2013.

Milestones of 'Team ICF'

ICF had completed glorious 60 years since inception and celebrated Diamond Jubilee in the year 2015 and had turned out 50,000 coaches, the highest by any rail coach manufacturer in the world and thirdly, dedicated a new unit to the Nation with state-of-the-art infrastructure to manufacture stainless steel LHB coaches. These 50,000 coaches turned out by ICF contain product-mix of more than 500 variants out of which a sizable number were exported. Today, ICF is the forerunner in the manufacture of self-propelled coaches viz. EMU and DEMU in the country

Projects Overview

Production of indigenously manufactured all steel all welded railway coaches started in the year 1955 with an installed capacity of 350 coaches per annum. The production was steadily increased duly augmenting infrastructure over the years and 1000 coaches were turned out in 1990. ICF surpassed the magic figure of 2000 coaches in 2016.

In tune with the policy of IR to completely switch over to stainless steel body coaches, ICF is gearing up to modernize the infrastructure. The new unit set-up to manufacture stainless steel LHB coaches has a capacity to turn out 300 coaches a year. This unit has the state-of-the-art infrastructure like Robotic spot welding centre, Robotic MIG welding centre, 5-axis machining centre, Laser cutting cum welding machine, other CNC machines, jigs and fixtures to manufacture stainless steel coaches with precise quality.
