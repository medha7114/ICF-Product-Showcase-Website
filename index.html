About Us

The Integral Coach Factory (ICF), established in 1955 and located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is a leading production unit of Indian Railways under the Ministry of Railways. Renowned for its high production capacity, ICF manufactures a diverse range of rail coaches, including passenger coaches, EMUS, DEMUs, and metro coaches. Notably, it developed the semi-high- speed Train 18 (Vande Bharat Express). With state-of-the-art workshops and a strong R&D wing, ICF focuses on innovation, safety, and passenger comfort.
